Medicinal Mushrooms

Flashcards & Study Guide

The health benefits of mushrooms are more than just woodsy folklore. Extensive scientific research has proven many mushrooms to be effective in treating or preventing a wide range of conditions.

The purpose of this webpage is to help students memorize the medicinal effects of well known mushrooms. Users can either build custom flashcards with the "front" and "back" forms below, or they can jump right into a full-length study guide containing all the information. This study guide can be sorted alphabetically by specific ailments or by each mushroom's scientific name.

Please note this is not a complete list of medicinal mushrooms and the ailments they treat.
